**DPSYC 503 — Learning: Theoretical Approaches and Educational Applications**  
Review of classical and contemporary theories of learning and their practical applications in educational settings.

**DPSYC 505 — Personality: Theoretical Approaches and Evolutionary Course**  
Survey of major personality theories from psychodynamic and trait perspectives to evolutionary and biological approaches.

**DPSYC 610-1 — Independent Research Study**  
Supervised independent research project at graduate level, supporting students in developing and executing original research in educational psychology.
