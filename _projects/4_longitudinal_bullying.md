This post-doctoral project, conducted at the Developmental Psychology Lab at the University of Cyprus, examined the parameters of bullying and cyberbullying across childhood and adolescence using a longitudinal design. The project involved leading research design, sampling procedures, instrument selection, and data analysis in collaboration with the lab team.

This was the inaugural project of a long-term research engagement at the Developmental Psychology Lab, laying the groundwork for subsequent funded projects on bullying and adolescent psychopathology.

**Funding:** University of Cyprus Internal Research Fund  
**Duration:** 2015–2017  
**Role:** Post-doctoral Fellow
